Easy Water Tracker

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Easy Water Tracker handles your information.

Easy Water Tracker is designed to help users build healthy hydration habits through water tracking, reminders, achievements, and wellness insights.

We are committed to keeping your information safe, simple, and transparent.

1. Information We Collect

Easy Water Tracker may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Water intake logs
  • Daily hydration goals
  • Reminder schedules
  • Wake and sleep times
  • Achievements and streak progress
  • Username and profile preferences
  • Dark mode and haptic settings
  • Measurement unit preferences (ml or oz)

2. Local Data Storage

Your hydration data is primarily stored locally on your device using secure local storage technologies.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Notifications

Easy Water Tracker may request notification permission to send hydration reminders throughout the day.

These reminders are scheduled based on your selected wake time, sleep time, and reminder interval settings.

4. Analytics and Advertising

Currently, Easy Water Tracker does not actively display advertisements.

In future updates, optional analytics or advertising services may be added to improve the app experience. If this occurs, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Third-Party Services

Easy Water Tracker may use third-party services such as:

  • Expo Notifications
  • Google Play Services
  • Crash reporting or analytics tools (future updates)

These services may collect limited technical information required for app functionality.

6. Children's Privacy

Easy Water Tracker is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information and maintain a secure app experience.

However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

8. Your Choices

You can:

  • Disable notifications anytime in device settings
  • Edit or remove your hydration data
  • Uninstall the app to remove locally stored information

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any future changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

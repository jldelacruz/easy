Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Easy Habit Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses, how it is stored, and your choices regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

Easy Habit Tracker is designed with a local-first approach. Most app information is stored directly on your device and is not sent to external servers.

The app may store the following information:

  • Username or profile information you enter
  • Habit names and settings
  • Completion history
  • Habit streak information
  • Reminder preferences
  • Avatar selections
  • App settings (notifications, haptics, onboarding status)

2. How Your Information Is Stored

Your data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage technologies. We do not currently operate cloud servers for storing your personal habit information.

Examples of locally stored information include:

  • Habit data
  • Progress history
  • Achievement status
  • Profile preferences
  • Reminder schedules

3. Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app may request notification permission. Notifications are used solely for:

  • Daily habit reminders
  • Scheduled habit alerts
  • Reminder updates

Notifications are generated locally on your device.

4. Analytics and Usage Calculations

Easy Habit Tracker performs calculations directly on your device to provide:

  • Streak tracking
  • Daily completion progress
  • Weekly completion percentages
  • Monthly heatmaps
  • Achievement progress

These calculations occur locally and are not transmitted externally.

5. Permissions We May Request

  • Notifications
    Used to send habit reminders if enabled.
  • Haptic/Vibration Access
    Used for optional feedback effects during habit completion.

6. Advertising

The app may include advertisements now or in future updates. Third-party advertising providers may collect limited anonymous information according to their own privacy policies.

Examples of possible information collected by advertising providers include:

  • Device information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Anonymous usage statistics

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party services used within the app.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal habit data with third parties.

8. Data Retention

Because data is stored locally on your device, information remains available until:

  • You delete specific habits
  • You clear app data
  • You uninstall the app

9. Data Deletion

Easy Habit Tracker is designed as a local-first application. Most user data, including habits, profile information, onboarding status, progress history, streaks, and settings, is stored directly on your device and not on our servers.

How Your Data Can Be Deleted

  • Deleting individual habits: Users may remove habits at any time through the app. Associated local completion history and reminder schedules related to that habit may also be removed.
  • Clearing app data: Users may remove all stored app data by clearing the application's storage through their device settings.
  • Uninstalling the app: Uninstalling Easy Habit Tracker typically removes all locally stored application data from the device, depending on your device and operating system behavior.
  • Profile and settings removal: Profile information, preferences, and locally stored settings are deleted when application data is cleared or the app is removed.

10. Children's Privacy

Easy Habit Tracker is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Future Features

Future versions of Easy Habit Tracker may introduce:

  • Cloud backup
  • Export features
  • Additional analytics
  • Advertising services

If these features affect privacy practices, this policy will be updated.

12. Changes To This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above.

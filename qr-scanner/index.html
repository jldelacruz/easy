Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 26, 2026

Easy QR Scanner (“we”, “our”, or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app collects, uses, and protects information when you use Easy QR Scanner.

Information We Collect

Easy QR Scanner is designed to minimize data collection. We do not collect personal information unless explicitly provided by the user.

  • Camera access for scanning QR codes and barcodes
  • Storage access for saving generated QR codes (optional)
  • Basic analytics and crash reports to improve app stability
  • Advertising identifiers for showing relevant ads through third-party ad services

How We Use Information

We may use collected information to:

  • Provide QR scanning and generation features
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Display advertisements

Third-Party Services

Easy QR Scanner may use third-party services such as:

  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

These services may collect anonymous usage information according to their own privacy policies.

Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Camera: Used to scan QR codes and barcodes
  • Storage: Used for saving or sharing QR images

Data Storage

QR scan history and generated QR codes may be stored locally on your device. We do not upload your personal QR data to our servers.

Data Deletion

Users may delete their stored data at any time by:

  • Clearing scan history within the app
  • Using the app’s delete/reset features
  • Uninstalling the application from the device
  • Clearing app storage through Android settings

If you wish to request deletion of any associated analytics data, you may contact us using the email below.

Children’s Privacy

Easy QR Scanner is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user information and maintain app security. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
